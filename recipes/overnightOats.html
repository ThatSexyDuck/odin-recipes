Overnight Oats

Delicious and Healthy Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon maple syurp

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into a sealable container and give it a stir to combine
  2. Let it soak in the fridge for at least 2 hours but ideally overnight for 8 hours
  3. Add your favourite toppings and eat up!
