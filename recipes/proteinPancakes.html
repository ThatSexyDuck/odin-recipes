Protein Pancakes

Perfect Protein-packed Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 75g oats
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • pinch of cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp protein powder
  • coconut oil
  • 1 tsp maple syrup

Steps

  1. Blend the banana, oats, eggs, milk, baking powder, protein powder and cinnamon in a blender until oats have broken down
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Ladle batter in portions sizes of your preference. cook for 1-2 minutes until bubbles begin to appear on the surface. Flip the pancake over and cook for a further minute until cooked through.
  3. add the maple syurp for topping (or another topping of your choice)
