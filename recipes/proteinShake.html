Almond Butter and Banana Protein Smmothie

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 cup full cream milk
  • 2 tablespoons almond butter
  • 2 tablespoons protein powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 5 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend until a smooth consistency
  2. Enjoy!
